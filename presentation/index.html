Design principles

Built like a team, not a tool.

Most "social media tools" make you do the work. We built ours to do the work for you, the way a senior analyst would.

Principle 01

Start from the deliverable

Every feature answers one question: "What is the client actually paying for?" If the output doesn't land on their desk as something they'd proudly show a board — we don't ship it.

Principle 02

Meet people where they are

Telegram, email, phone voice. Never ask an agency team to learn a new UI. Intelligence meets you inside WhatsApp-speed workflows.

Principle 03

Never fail silently

6-layer fallback: if the primary AI is down, four others step in automatically. If all AI fails, a cloud backup catches it. Zero blank reports. Ever.
